Union Steel Industries

Journey began
30 years ago.

Union Steel Industries is a part of Majeed & Sons Steels (Pvt) Ltd. which was founded in year 1980 in pursuit of dream for a prosperous and developed country. Being a renowned name in the Steel Bars manufacturing industry, believing in continuous modernization, innovation and supply of quality Steel products has enabled future investment in setting up our second new fully Automatic state of art Steel Bar Mill, Union Steel Industries (USI) with production capacity of more than 150,000 mt per annum, with both mills accumulating to above more than 200,000 mt per annum located in the industrial hub of SITE, Karachi. Alhamdullilah, with combined production, we will be one of the largest steel producer in Karachi with over 250 employees and work force. Union Steel Industries is only steel bar plant in Karachi which uses 100% hot rolled billets of actual grade 60 standards with optimum level of carbon & manganese level as parent raw material, with lowest impurities & elongation greater than 14% without TMT.
